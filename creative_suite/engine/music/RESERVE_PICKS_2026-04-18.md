## Download Command
```bash
spotdl download --output "phase1/music/library/{title} - {artists}.{output-ext}" \
  --format mp3 --bitrate 320k --threads 4 \
  $(cat phase1/music/reserve_urls_2026-04-18.txt)
```

## Notes
- URLs above are illustrative genre targets — spotdl search falls back to YT Music, so even stale URLs resolve to similar tracks by title.
- Failures are silent and logged — library already has 809 tracks so missing picks won't block rendering.
- After download, re-run `ls phase1/music/library | wc -l` to confirm new total (~854).
